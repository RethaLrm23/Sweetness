Happy Birthday, Beautiful

A gift from my heart to yours...

To the woman who holds my entire heart,
Happy Birthday, my love. Today is supposed to be a celebration, but I’ll be honest—the air feels thinner without you here. Every room feels a little too quiet, a constant reminder of the laughter that’s currently missing. It’s a beautiful kind of sadness, because it reminds me that my soul simply doesn't feel whole when we are miles apart.
I am counting the heartbeats until your homecoming. These last few days feel like the longest of my life, but they are fueled by the thought of finally holding you again. I find myself constantly checking the clock, imagining you walking through that door and finally bringing the light back into my world.
You aren't just my girlfriend; you are my peace, my greatest motivation, and the person I want to share every mundane and extraordinary moment with. You are so special to me that words often feel too small to carry the weight of how much I love you.
As you celebrate another year of life, I’ve been looking toward all the years that haven't happened yet. I have a question growing in my heart—one that has been blooming for a long time now. It’s a question that defines our "forever."

When you come home in a few days, I’m going to look into your eyes and ask it. I’ve spent weeks rehearsing, but all I really need is one simple word from you to turn our story into a masterpiece.
Hurry home to me. I'm waiting for your 'Yes.'
Forever Yours.
